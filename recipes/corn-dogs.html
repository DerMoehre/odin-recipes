Corn Dogs

back

Description

I love the corn dogs you buy at carnivals but wanted to make them at home so I created this easy batter for frankfurters. Great served with mustard.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal and flour
  • 1 pinch of sugar
  • 1 pinch of baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • Oil to fry the corndogs
  • 16 beef frankfurters

Directions

Step 1

Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirr in the milk and egg

Step 2

Skewer the frankfurters and roll in batter

Step 3

Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly brown
